Here are 5 Authentic and Fabricated Hadiths and the reasons why they are either Authentic or fabricated.

1. Seeking Knowledge

Authentic:"Whoever follows a path in pursuit of knowledge, Allah will make easy for him a path to Paradise."

Fabricated:"Seek knowledge even if its in China." - Classified as Fabricated by scholars like ibn Jawzi.

Note: The authentic hadith empahsizes the virtue of seeking knowledge, while the fabricated one lacks a reliable chain of narration.

2. Companions as Stars

Authentic: "Hold fast to my sunnah and the sunnah of the rightly guided Caliphs."

Fabricated: "My Companions are like stars; whichever of them you follow you will be rightly guided." - Declared fabricated by scholars due to weak chains.

Note: The fabricated hadith lacks authentic chains and is not found in major collections.

3. Heart of the Qur'an

Authentic: "Everything has a heart and the heart of the Qur'an is Ya-Sin." - Found in Tirmidhi, though some scholars debate its authenticity.

Fabricated: "Whoever reads surah Ya-Sin once will have the reward of reading the Qur'an ten times."- Classified as fabricated by scholars like ibn Abi hatim.

Note: Exaggerated rewards without authentic chains are signs of fabrication.

4. Visiting Graves

Authentic: "I had forbidden you to visit graves but now you may visit them." (Sahih Muslim)

Fabricated: Whoever visits the grave of his parents every Friday will have his sins forgiven." - Deemed fabricated due to lack of authentic chains

Note: Fabricated hadiths often promise specific rewards without basis in authentic sources.

5. Diversity as Mercy

Authentic: Emphasis on unity and division is found in multiple authentic hadiths.

Fabricated: "Diversity among my followers is a mercy." - Not found in authentic collections and considered fabricated.
