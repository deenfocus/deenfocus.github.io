Story of prophet Adam A.S

1. Creation of Adam

Allah created Adam A.S from clay and breathed into him His spirit, Making him his first human being.

Qur'an (Surah Sad 38:71-72):
"When your lord said to the angels, 'I am creating a human being from clay... then i fashioned him and breathedinto him My spirit, so fall down in prostration to him."

2. Command to Angels

Allah ordered all the angels to prostrate before Adam. All obeyed except Iblis, who refused and became proud

Qur'an (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:34):
"And [Mention] when we said to the angels, 'Prostrate before Adam'; so they prostrated except Iblis"

3. Adam in Paradise

Adam and hawwa were placed in paradise witheverything allowed except one tree.

Qur'an(Surah Al-Baqarah 2:35):
"O Adam, dwell, you and your wife, in paradise and eat... but do not approach this tree"

4. The Deception

Iblis decieved the, leading them to eat from the forbidden tree. Their shame became visible and theywere sent out of paradise.

Qur'an (Al-A'raf 7:20-22):
"Then Satan whispered to them... So he cause them to fall through deception..."

5. Repentance and Mercy

They repentedand Allah forgave them, Teaching them the words of repentance.

Qur'an (Surah Al-Baqarah :37)
"Then Adam received from his words, and He accepted his repentance"

6. Life on Earth

Allahsent them to earth and promise guidance to mankind.

Qur'an (Surah TaHa 20:123) "Descend on Paradise-all of you. then when guidance comes..."
